ABOUT ESTHER

Esther Wojcicki

Leading American educator, journalist, and mother. Founder of the Media Arts program at Palo Alto High School — from 20 students in 1984 to over 600, with nine award-winning publications and one of the largest journalism programs in the nation.

Vice Chair of Creative Commons. Instrumental in shaping GoogleEdu since its inception. California Teacher of the Year. Advisor to the U.S. Department of Education, Carnegie Foundation, Hewlett Foundation, and Time Magazine.
